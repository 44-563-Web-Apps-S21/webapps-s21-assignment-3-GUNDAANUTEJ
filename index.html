Making paper boat

  1. List of items
    • sheet of paper letter sized or A4.
    • Scissor.
  2. Regular print paper is perfect for this project.
  3. Use as many colors as possible.
    1. Red
    2. Blue
    3. Green
    4. white
  4. Start off with a sheet of paper and fold it in half downwards.
  5. Fold in half again as shown on the second image.
  6. Bring corners to the center so that you get a triangle shape with a rectangle under it.
  7. Fold the rectangle part under the triangle upwards and its done.
